Asteroid Escape

Made with: Shadertoy (WebGL)
Source: Shadertoy

Solar System Screensaver was a project I made for CS44N: Great Ideas in Graphics. It was my introduction to using shaders and an OpenGL language. It was very difficult to learn how to use this tool - it was very advanced relative to the technical experience I had at the time. I managed to make a representation of the solar system using simple polygonal squares, using trig functions to map the orbits of planets

The scale of this model: The Sun is 1/4th its size, the solid planets are 2 times their sizes, and the gas planets are to scale. The times of orbit are accurate
