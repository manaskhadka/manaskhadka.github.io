About Me

Writing code has become my creative and inspirational outlet, but the core of my values is what brought me where I am. Here, I hope to showcase the little and big things that make me who I am.

Science Olympiad

A huge influence on my values and who I am today stems from the 7 years I spent doing science olympiad. I got the opportunity to explore so many branches of science. From geology to epidemiology, I got absorbed in the amazing world around me, and through that I developed a strong appreciation for interdisciplinary science. However, I value my time in science olympiad more so for the memories it gave me with some extraordinary people.
